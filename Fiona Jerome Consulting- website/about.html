About

Fiona Jerome is a director of brand marketing, with experience managing global teams and multimillion-dollar campaigns. Her background in strategy, visual design, and account management informs her mindful but competitive approach with an eye for the customer's needs.

Fiona is fueled by her passion for understanding the nuances of cross-cultural advertising and visual design. She considers herself a "forever student," eager to both build on her academic foundations in psychology and sociology, and stay in tune with the latest digital marketing strategies through continued coursework and professional development.

Fiona's hunger for knowledge and determination to consult for internationally succesful companies has vastly improved the productivity of her company by implementing strategic project management methods and ensuring a work-life balance. Madison believes mindfulness in the workplace is key to success—a tenet she lives out through her interests in painting, reading, gardening, and traveling.

Fiona is currently working as a freelance consulting director and is always interested in a challenge. Reach out to fionajerome@fionajeromeconsulting.com to connect!
